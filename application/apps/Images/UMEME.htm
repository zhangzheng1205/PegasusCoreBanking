Umeme has disconnected the power of over 13,000 customers in Kabalagala, a Kampala suburb after discovering that over 70% of the power connections are illegal.

UMEME ANNOUNCES DECREASED LOAD SHEDDING OVER HOLIDAY SEASON December 6, 2011

UMEME has altered the load-shedding structure to prioritise electricity supply for Domestic Customers and Small & Medium Scale Enterprises (SMEs), in order to increase availability of supply for more customers during the Christmas season.

Domestic Customers will now enjoy uninterrupted electricity for two nights in a row broken by a load-shedding interval of only 12 hours on the third night.
